Welcome to Dojo Sushi Burrito

Dojo Sushi Burrito is a fast casual sushi restaurant found in the heart of the Central Center of Somewhere City, Somehwere State.

It’s streamlined menu offers a variety of fresh and healthy options from delicious Sushi Burritos, Chef Special Bowls, Sushi Rolls, Soups and Desserts with exquisite flavors that will blow your taste buds away!

Since Dojo Sushi Burrito's opening in 2015, there has been no other place like it. People have traveled from all over Southern Somewhere State to have their Sushi Burrito and it has become the best Sushi Burrito restaurant in Southern Somewhere State.

We invite you to join us and experience it for yourself.

Our Goal

Dojo Sushi Burrito is committed to uniting fresh, locally grown produce with farm-raised and wild-caught seafood to make the freshest dishes you’ll find anywhere. Our fish is fresh, never frozen, and delivered to our doors daily.
