Puerto Princesa

Puerto Princesa is the 2nd most popular vacation destination in the province, behind El Nido. Recognized as the cleanest and greenest city in the Philippines, the capital of Palawan offers a pleasant urban environment nestled in splendid natural surroundings that include great beaches and pristine forested hills.

Puerto Princesa is the main arrival hub by plane and ferry to Palawan Island. Manila is about an hour-long flight away.
